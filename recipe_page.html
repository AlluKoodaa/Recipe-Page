Allu's Scrambled Eggs

Part of a healthy breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • Tbsp Butter/Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dash Smoked chili sauce (optional)
  • Chives (optional)

Preparation

  1. Break the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Mix the eggs with a fork. Add seasoning and shredded chives (optional)
  3. Heat a frying pan to medium heat. Add butter/cooking oil. Let the butter melt/cooking oil heat.
  4. Pour the scrambled eggs to the middle of the frying pan. Let it set and then start slowly scraping the scrambled eggs with a spatula. Go from edge to edge until done.
  5. Serve on toasted bread.
  6. Enjoy!
